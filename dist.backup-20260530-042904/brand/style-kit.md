# tinkerbench Style Kit

## Brand Summary
tinkerbench is a hands-on affiliate site for DIYers, makers, garage tinkerers, and weekend builders. The identity feels like a clean workbench with sawdust on graph paper.

**Tagline:** Tools that earn their spot on the bench.

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex |
|---|---|
| primary | #E0691A |
| secondary | #1F2329 |
| accent | #D4B47A |
| background | #F4E8D0 |
| text | #1F2329 |
| muted | #8A7358 |

Body-text contrast: `#1F2329` on `#F4E8D0` = **13.00:1**.

## Type Pairing Recommendation
Wordmark/display: JetBrains Mono, Cousine, or Oswald-style condensed sans. UI/body: Inter or Roboto for instructions and buying guides.

## Voice & Tone Notes
Hands-on, a little nerdy, generous, and practical. Recommend the cheap version when it actually works.

## Do
- Use workshop colors, grid-paper cues, and tool-like structure.
- Explain tradeoffs clearly and show useful shortcuts.
- Keep copy friendly but technically grounded.

## Don't
- Do not turn the brand into macho contractor cosplay.
- Do not clutter pages with grungy textures.
- Do not hide budget-friendly picks.
